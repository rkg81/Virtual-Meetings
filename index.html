Meal Plan Overview

Understanding Your Options

Our meal plans offer flexibility and variety to suit every student's needs. Freshmen students who will live on-campus are automatically added to an on-campus meal plan.

Special Meal Options For International Students:

The Special Meal Plan is designed to provide international students with affordable and flexible meal choices that cater to their specific needs — allowing a balance of on-campus dining and cooking for themselves.

Dorm Check-In Process

What to expect on arrival
  1. Official Check-In Time
    Check-ins will begin at 9:00 AM on Monday, December 1, 2025. All students should report to Wyly Tower 227 to pick up their key.
  2. Verify Room Assignment (StarRez)
    Log in to your StarRez account before arriving to see which room has been assigned to you.
  3. Early Arrival Options
    1. Book Motel: If you are arriving early, you may book a motel for your stay till check-in date. Here are some motel options in Ruston such as Super 8 by Wyndham Ruston. Click here to see more options.
    2. Special Permission: If you have special permission from Housing department (e.g., approved early check-in), you might collect your dorm keys from the LA Tech Police Department. Please contact the housing deparment whether you have to pay any additional fee if you have special permission for early check-in.

Mailing Address for Students

How to receive mail & packages successfully

[Recipient's First and Last Name] (Required — must match Student ID name)

Ruston, LA 71272

Package Collection Centre Policy

Students must present their student ID card to pick up packages. For security and privacy, only the person to whom the package is addressed may receive the package.
